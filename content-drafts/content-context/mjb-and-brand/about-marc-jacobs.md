- **1993:** After Jacobs was fired from Perry Ellis for his infamous grunge collection, he and Duffy launched Marc Jacobs Duffy Designs Inc. to independently produce and license the label.

Born in NYC in 1963, Marc Jacobs graduated from Parsons School of Design, where he was the youngest designer ever to receive the CFDA's Perry Ellis Award for New Fashion Talent.  
  
In 1992, Jacobs showed his grunge collection for Perry Ellis. In April 1994 he successfully debuted his eponymous collection. Since then, he's spearheaded countless successful ready-to-wear, handbag, fragrance, cosmetic & book launches.  
  
A cultural icon, Marc Jacobs continues to celebrate individuality and authenticity through bold designs and distinguished collaborations.
